Welcome to Straight Fin Farms — a 34-acre private waterfront retreat just 8 minutes from downtown Bobcaygeon and 1.5 hours from Toronto. The farmhouse sleeps 16+ guests across 7 uniquely designed bedrooms and 4.5 bathrooms, with a full kitchen, spacious living areas, and wraparound outdoor spaces.

The property includes 500 feet of private waterfront with kayaks and a pedal boat, an outdoor hot tub, fireplace, BBQ, forest trails, a developing permaculture farm with gardens, orchards, beehives, and mushroom cultivation areas. Whether you're here for a multi-family getaway, a milestone birthday, a yoga retreat, or a corporate offsite — the entire property is yours.

Bobcaygeon is one of the Kawarthas' most charming small towns. You're just minutes from restaurants, craft shops, Kawartha Dairy ice cream, boat launches on the Trent-Severn Waterway, and the famous Thursday night concerts on the bridge all summer long. The property is four-season — summer bonfires, fall harvest colours, winter snowshoeing and hot tub evenings, spring planting season. Every visit is different.

Cancellation Policy & Security Deposit

Cancellation Policy

Firm (Short-Term Stays) — Full refund if cancelled at least 30 days before check-in. Partial refund if cancelled 7–30 days before check-in.

Long-Term (30+ Days) — Full refund if cancelled within 48 hours of booking and at least 28 days before check-in. After that, the first 30 days of the stay are non-refundable.

Security Deposit

A $500 refundable hold protects against accidental damages. Here's how it works:

1. At booking — You securely save your card on file via Stripe. No charge, no hold.

2. 1–2 days before arrival — Stripe places a $500 authorization hold on your saved card. This is not a charge; the funds are temporarily reserved (your bank may show it as "pending") but no money is transferred.

3. After checkout — A routine property inspection is performed. If everything is in order, the hold is released within 7 days (often within 24 hours). If damages or extra cleaning are needed, only the corresponding amount is captured from the hold and an itemized statement is sent.
